# Opportunities

We are always interested in hearing from thoughtful, motivated people who want to do impactful translational cancer research.

## Openings
**Current status:** Informational / pre-launch phase (formal postings coming later).

## Who we’re looking for
- Graduate students, postdocs, research staff
- Strong interest in translational science, biomarkers, and/or multi-omics
- Comfort with either wet lab, computational analysis, or both

## How to reach out
Email **info@furuyalab.org** with:
- CV
- 2–3 sentences about your interests
- A paper/project you’re proud of

We’ll respond as quickly as we can.
